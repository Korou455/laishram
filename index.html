About

This Agro Market Information System is built to support farmers with simple and practical tools for daily farming activities. It provides clear guidance on animal feeding, growth stages, and care management. Farmers can also use the platform to calculate values, check important numbers, and sell their products by uploading images with price and contact details. The system is designed to improve productivity, reduce confusion, and make farming more organized, efficient, and accessible for both beginners and experienced users.
